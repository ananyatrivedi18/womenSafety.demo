Safety Tips

Simple, practical habits that help you stay aware and prepared.

01
📍

Share Your Location

Let a trusted person track your live location when travelling alone, especially at night.

02
👀

Stay Aware

Keep your head up and avoid distractions like loud headphones in unfamiliar areas.

03
🔋

Charged Phone

Keep your phone charged and carry a power bank so help is always reachable.

04
🚕

Verify Your Ride

Check the driver, car, and plate before getting in. Share trip details with someone.

05
💪

Trust Your Instincts

If a situation feels wrong, leave. Your intuition is a powerful safety tool.

06
📞

Know Emergency Numbers

Memorize local emergency numbers and keep key contacts on speed dial.

07
🗺️

Plan Your Route

Choose well-lit, busy streets and tell someone your expected arrival time.

08
🔑

Keys Ready

Have your keys out before reaching your door or car to avoid lingering.
